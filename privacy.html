Peaked App - Privacy Policy


Your privacy is our top priority. We have designed our app with your privacy in mind, and we want to be transparent about how we handle your personal data.

Last Updated: 9 May 2023

Personal Data Collection and Use

Peaked does not collect, store, or use any personal data. All information entered into the app remains on your device, and none of it is transmitted or shared with us or any third parties.

No Tracking

We do not track or monitor your usage of the Peaked app. The app does not use cookies, analytics, or other tracking technologies. Your app experience is entirely private and secure.

No Third-Party Sharing

As we do not collect any personal data, we do not share or sell any information with third parties for advertising or any other purposes.

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device, and we do not have access to it. We recommend that you regularly back up your device to prevent data loss.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website or within the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the Peaked app, please contact us at hi@peaked.today
